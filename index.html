Our history

Four your musings, a small overview by the founder.

  • Somewhere during 2013

    Before it all began

    This story begins when I was pitching the idea of NoTimeForInternet.com: an online service to handle all internet communication, suggesting valid answers , trends on twitter, recent news, ...

  • November 2013

    Planting a seed

    I tried selling it to everyone I knew, and so I had the following conversation during a dinner with a business mentor:

    • Would you pay for this service?
    • No, I don't have time for internet, even with a tool.
      You know what I'd pay real money for? You answering prospect questions and providing me new leads.
    • But you're in finance, so I can't properly handle all of that.
    • That's your problem; offer this, and I'll buy it.

  • February 2014

    An idea is emerging

    The conversation we had kept lingering around in my head, and I started looking for a solution. After pivotting my point-of-view for so many times, it hit me like a rock: while I probably couldn't provide him any leads, there might be others in my network that I could provide leads, so I started searching...
