Professional Sunroom Designer

A simplified CAD built for sales to design sunrooms and generate quotes.

This CAD application - teachable in minutes, not hours -, allows your sales people to generate a plan and a realtime visualization of the design in the customer's existing context (f.e. a back wall.)

The specifications in this design are detailed enough for a commercial plan, and combined by the customer with some other details to generate a detailed quote, so this is a fully automated process. The CAD guy now only has spend 5 minutes reviewing the design, as opposed to 1 or more hours drawing it in order to generate an accurate quote.

Using a regular smartphone, or a special 360 degrees camera, sales people can take a surround picture of the garden, and project the summer house extension on the surround view in real-time. This will allow the prospect to see how well the design fits in the current situation.

  • Date: August 2015
  • Category: Sales, visualisation, quote generation
